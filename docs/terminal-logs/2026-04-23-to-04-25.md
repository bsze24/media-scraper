**Round 2 (bugbot's follow-up review on round 1):**
3. `fix(transcript): merge mount effects to fix speed restore race` ‚Äî two `useEffect`s with `[]` deps were racing on mount; the sessionStorage hydration silently overwrote the saved-view speed restore. Fixed in both `TranscriptViewer.tsx` (client) and `page.tsx`'s `generateMetadata` (server) to keep precedence consistent across OG previews and live viewer.
